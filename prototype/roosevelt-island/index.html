Every object of public consequence on this island has a passport. A passport answers five questions, in this order:

  • 1  What is this?
  • 2  Who is responsible for it?
  • 3  Who paid for it?
  • 4  What is happening to it now?
  • 5  What has happened to it over its lifetime?

Seven objects here are fully researched. Twenty-two are stubs, and say so. Nothing is invented: where the public record has no answer, the passport says so and names what is missing.

Objects drawn in red have a finding. Start with the Renwick Ruin, the Tram, or AVAC.
